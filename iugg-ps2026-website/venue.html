Conference Venue

The symposium will take place in Wuhan, a major city located at the confluence of the Yangtze and Han rivers and a leading center for higher education and scientific research in China.

Detailed information about the exact venue, accommodation options, and local transportation will be provided in due course. Participants are encouraged to check this page regularly for updates.

Travel Information

  • International Arrival: Wuhan can be reached via major international hubs in Asia and beyond, with domestic connections to Wuhan Tianhe International Airport.
  • High-Speed Rail: Wuhan is a key node of China's high-speed rail network, with convenient connections to cities such as Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, and Chengdu.
  • Local Transportation: The city offers an extensive metro and bus network, as well as taxis and ride-hailing services.
